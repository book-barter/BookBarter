BookBarter is a book exchange platform designed to help people--especially students and families who cannot afford new books--get access to the books they need. It allows users to exchange old books for new ones rather than throwing them away. This promotes both affordability and sustainability by reducing waste and giving books a longer life.

üí° Why is it Helpful?

After reading, many people find their books become useless. Instead of letting them collect dust or go to waste, users can submit those books for exchange. The app makes the process simple and fair. Users only pay the difference in value between the old and new books, based on verified conditions--not claimed ones. It saves money and encourages reuse.

Users submit a request to exchange their old book. Our team verifies the book's condition and then applies a depreciation formula to determine its current value. The new book is given based on this verified value--not on user input.
